1. Introduction

The Badminton Court Management System ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Philippines' Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA 2012).

2. Data We Collect

When you use Facebook Login or other OAuth providers, we collect:

  • Public Profile Information (Name, Profile Picture)
  • Email Address

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to facilitate account creation, provide user authentication, and manage court bookings. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve ads. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Retention and Deletion (Facebook Requirements)

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your data and revoke app permissions, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Profile's Settings & Privacy > Settings.
  2. Look for Apps and Websites and select Badminton Court.
  3. Click the Remove button.
  4. Alternatively, contact us at solomiosisante@gmail.com to request manual data deletion from our servers.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy concerns or data access requests, contact our Data Protection Officer at solomiosisante@gmail.com.
